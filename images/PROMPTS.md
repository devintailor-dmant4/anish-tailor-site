## 1. `hero-bg.jpg`
**Used in:** Hero section — full-bleed background behind the headline

**What it must say without words:**
Scale. Infrastructure. Something real was built here. This is serious work.

**AI Prompt (Midjourney / DALL-E / Firefly):**
> Aerial photography of a large industrial water treatment facility at golden hour,
> circular clarifiers and settling tanks visible from above, pipes and infrastructure,
> warm orange and teal light, dramatic shadows, photorealistic, cinematic quality,
> no people, ultra wide angle, high detail --ar 16:9

**Stock photo search (Unsplash / Shutterstock / Getty):**

Filter: Landscape, high resolution
